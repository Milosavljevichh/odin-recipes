Rainbow Pasta Salad II

This is a really quick and easy pasta salad.
Great for picnics and BBQ's.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package tri-colored pasta assortment
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

Cooking Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse in cold water.
  3. In a large bowl combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, cooled pasta and Italian dressing. Refrigerate overnight, or for at least 1 hour.
