American Pancakes


Fluffy Homemade American Pancakes

The following recipe is quite easy, suitable for
begginers and very easy to follow!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 glasses of milk (200ml)
  • 1/3 glass of oil
  • 1/3 glass of sugar
  • 2 vanilla sugars
  • 2,5 glasses of flour

Cooking Steps

  1. Seperate the yolks from the whites
  2. Mix the whites into a strong snow and
    for now, leave them aside
  3. Mix the yolks with the sugar
  4. Add the milk and the rest of ingredients
  5. Mix everything together
  6. At the end, add the mixed whites
  7. Pour the mix into a pan and fry it on a
    medium temperature
